Quiz Instructions

Welcome to the General Knowledge Quiz!

Here’s how the quiz works:

  • You will be asked a series of general knowledge questions.
  • Each question has four possible answers.
  • Click on the answer you think is correct.
  • If your answer is correct, it will turn green.
  • If your answer is wrong, it will turn red.
  • The correct answer will always be highlighted after you choose.
  • Your score will increase each time you answer correctly.
  • The progress bar shows how far you are through the quiz.
  • Click "Next" to move to the next question.
  • At the end, your final score will be shown.

Good luck and have fun!
